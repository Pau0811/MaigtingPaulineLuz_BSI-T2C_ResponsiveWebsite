Marinduque Map

The province of Marinduque is called "The Heart of the Philippines".
Marinduque had (6) municipalities, eventhough the Marinduque is not too large province.

Main Municipality of Marinduque

The (6) municipalities of Marinduque are Boac, Mogpog, Sta. Cruz, Torrijos, Buenavista and Gasan.
The main municipality is the Boac.
